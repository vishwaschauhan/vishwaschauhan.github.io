Introduction of the Institute with the Mission Statement and Object

“we are here by choice not by chance”

The ethics of our institutes is expressed in few words written below and is self illuminating, the institution work on the visualization that “we may never have the opportunity to do great things in a great way ,but we all have the chance to do small things in a great way”. The changing scenario of education has enhanced the enormity and importance of the task of college to educate the students such that it gives direction and perspective to their thoughts. The onus of inculcating scruples in the dynamic world where ethics have slipped into oblivion owing to globalization, liberalization and westernization rests with educational institutions. It is sobering responsibility of an educational institution to train, develop the knowledge, mind and character of the students.
